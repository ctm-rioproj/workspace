Services

As a disaster relief group, we provide a wide variety of services. However, we specialize in restoring power and communication lines, and can dispatch a team to most locations within 8 hours.

Restoration of internet connectivity and telecommunications. Usually done temporarily via satellite internet while the major repairs are being done.
Provision of electricity for heating, cooling, and lighting. Generators are used as a temporary measure, and our technicians repair wiring faults and damage within homes and businesses.
Cosmetic and structural repairs for houses, such as door and drywall repair, window replacement, and roof repair.
Repairs of businesses and external structures such as carports and sheds.
Clearing of debris and wreckage, including downed trees, power lines, and flood debris.
