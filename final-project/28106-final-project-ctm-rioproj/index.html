Welcome to the Disaster Recovery Group homepage.

We are a US-based nonprofit organization focused on repairs following natural disasters and manmade accidents in remote and low-income communities. From homes to roads to businesses, we believe everyone deserves a helping hand in times of crisis.

We employ a devoted team of volunteer workers from all over the country, with skills in construction, demolition, plumbing, painting and more. Our organization also has partnered with several construction and restoration companies willing to go wherever it takes to help communities rebound.


We specialize in restoring power and communication infrastructure, though there are many other services we provide. If you were referred to this site following an accident and would like to request aid, follow this link: Aid Request Form
